General information

  • Class time is Mon., Wed. and Fri. from 1:30-3:30 pm.

  • TA office hours are Mondays 3:30-4:30 pm outside at the cafe at LKSC.

  • Monday and Wednesday are lectures. Friday is an in-class workshop where students work in groups on problem sets in class. Before the workshop, students should have already been working on their problems before hand so they come to class with questions.
